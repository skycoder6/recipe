The Recipe for Damper

- Ingredients

  • 3 Cups of plain white flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or margarine
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 baking tray greased with oil

- The Method

  1. Wash you hands.
  2. Make sure you have a trusted adult for the cutting and the oven.
  3. Sift together flour, salt and baking soda.
  4. With your fingers, mix in butter or margarine until it crumbles.
  5. Make a hole right in the middle of the mixture and then pour the milk all at once.
    Stir it together until it forms a big lump.
  6. Sprinkle plenty of flour on your fingers and pat the dough into a circle about
    3 or 4 centimetres thick.
  7. Place the dough on a greased baking tray.
  8. With a sharp knife, carefully mark a big X on top of the dough halfway through it.
  9. Bake the dough at 230°C in the oven for 10 minutes.
    Turn the temperature down to 170°C and cook for another 20-30 minutes
    or until the bread is a light golden-brown colour.
  10. Slightly cool, cut it into pieces,
    add butter, margarine or honey or nothing at all.
    Let yourself enjoy a delicious treat! :b

- Fun Fact

Damper can be made in a campfire, it is an excellent source of food for camping
